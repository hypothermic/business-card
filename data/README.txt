================================================================================
=======                         Instructions                             =======
================================================================================

This device can be used as a Bluetooth LE input method!

To pair it with your computer:

    1. Connect with "MB Business Card".

    2. If your computer asks you to confirm a pin code, click confirm.

    3. Press and hold the 'Volume Up' and 'Volume Down' buttons on
       the business card simultaneously for 3 seconds.

    4. Done!

================================================================================
=======                            Modes                                 =======
================================================================================

This device has two modes.
The device's mode dictates which keystroke is sent when a button is tapped.

    - Media Controls mode (default):
        The buttons function normally.
        
    - Navigation Controls mode:
        - Volume Up is remapped to Up
        - Volume Down is remapped to Down
        - Play/Pause is remapped to Left
        - Mute is remapped to Right

To switch between modes, press and hold the 'Play/Pause' button for 5 seconds.
Do not touch other buttons during this time.

================================================================================
=======                         Status LEDs                              =======
================================================================================

The Green LED blinks...
    -  twice per second when a pairing request is pending.
    -  once when pairing was successful.

The Red LED indicates that a device has disconnected. (gracefully or not)

The Blue LED blinks when a touch input is registered.
